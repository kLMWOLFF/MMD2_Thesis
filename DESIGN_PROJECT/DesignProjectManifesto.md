# Interactive Futures

Designing for what comes next, grounded in today’s social/ technological/cultural realities

## Concepts

### ARBORESCENCE
- Narrative structure (storytelling)
- Clear outline of the possible choices player/user can make
- Identify when to communicate
- How each action can be triggered (sound, gestures, actions, feedback)

![alt text](<IMG_ref/TOUCH - Frame2.jpg>)

### AGENCY
- Power to Influence and Change
- Control
- Ability to make meaningfull decisions


### EMBODIMENT
- Embody agency (gesture, movement, action)
- How to interact:

        - What is happening in my scenario?
        - What are my intentions?
        - Who are the key actors?
        - Where does it happen?
        - What are the main interactions?

![alt text](<IMG_ref/TOUCH - Frame3.jpg>)

### INTERACTION (Points of contact inventory)
#### User + Object/Interface

        - How do we interact with the story?
        - What is the key interaction?
        - how does your interface acts/responds?
        - how do the player get feedback?
        - what happens when we make a choice/action?
        - Main functionalities?
        - How do people control the object/Can they control it?
        - Is the object controlled by external parameters? Which ones?
