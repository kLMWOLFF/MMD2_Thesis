# Tactile VR

The objective of this prototype is to build a treasure hunt game VR game with no graphics, where player is guided mainly by tactile feedbacks and some audio cues.
The VR headset is used only to gather the player position as it moves around the physical space and deliver audio cues. The controllers are used to track hand position and deliver haptic feedback.
The idea is to explore mainly haptics in the first prototype, then move to more complex, multi stimuly involving, heat, pressure, humidity, etc (connecting VR game and arduino sensors). The audio will be used as a helping mechanic for navigation, but most feedback must remain tactile.
