## Research Question: 
                Touching the digital: How to redesign digital touch?
**How to redesign touch through hybrid experiences?**
Touching the digital (Tangible user interface): being able to feel digital textures (haptic devices)

### Resume: 
This research aims to explore the potential of the sense of touch in everyday digital interactions, exploring the physical embodiment of information and how touch is perceived and understood across digital environments (video games, digital prototyping [digital twins] and UI) and its tools.

Through a combination of research in digital textures and materials through case studies, sensory ethnography and speculative design, it investigates the language of touch and proposes new communication channels through "augmented touch".
